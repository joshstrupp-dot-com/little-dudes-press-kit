About the Game

Little Dudes is a mellow shopkeeping sim where you sell succulents, snacks, and other essentials to a stream of endearing little creatures—each more awkwardly adorable than the last. Set in the hand-drawn world of Dudetopia, the game invites players to pick a shop, stock its shelves, upgrade its looks, and gently serve their community with no pressure, no timers, and no chaos. It's a point-and-click world built for those with full hearts and tired brains. Watch your shop come to life as shelves fill and the dudes keep showing up, hungry for goods and good vibes.
